Overview

Set in the dangerous virtual world of Gun Gale Online (GGO), the Phantom Bullet Arc follows Kirito as he investigates mysterious deaths occurring in the game that mirror real-life fatalities. Tasked with uncovering the truth, Kirito must adapt to a new, gun-focused combat system and face a deadly foe, Death Gun.

Partnering with Sinon, a skilled sniper with her own haunting past, Kirito dives into a high-stakes virtual tournament to uncover Death Gun's identity and put a stop to his reign of terror.

"In GGO, the only way to survive is to pull the trigger before your enemy does."

Story Breakdown

The arc begins with Kirito being recruited by a government agency to investigate the strange phenomenon of players being killed in real life after being shot by the mysterious "Death Gun" in GGO.

Adopting a new avatar, Kirito enters the tournament known as the Bullet of Bullets, where he meets Sinon, a sharpshooter with unmatched precision and a traumatic backstory.

"No matter how dark the world becomes, there's always a light worth fighting for."

Together, Kirito and Sinon navigate the treacherous tournament, uncovering clues about Death Gun's methods and true identity. The investigation leads to a thrilling confrontation both in-game and in the real world, where Kirito must use all his skills to save lives and bring justice to the victims.

Major Characters

Kirito

Kirito enters Gun Gale Online with a new avatar and faces unique challenges as he adapts to a world dominated by firearms. Key Role: Investigates Death Gun and protects his fellow players.

Sinon

Sinon is a skilled sniper who partners with Kirito. Her tragic past fuels her resolve to become stronger in both the virtual and real worlds. Key Role: Supports Kirito and faces her own inner demons.

Death Gun

A terrifying presence in GGO, Death Gun's identity and methods are shrouded in mystery. He serves as the main antagonist of the arc. Key Role: Orchestrates in-game murders that affect real-life players.
