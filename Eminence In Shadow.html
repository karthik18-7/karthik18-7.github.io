Overview

The Eminence in Shadow follows Cid Kagenou, a young man who has been reincarnated into a fantasy world. In his past life, he was just an ordinary person, but in this new life, he adopts the persona of "Shadow" and dreams of becoming a mastermind. His goal is to secretly lead an organization that fights against an evil cult, even though he believes the cult is purely fictional. Cid forms a group called Shadow Garden to take down the cult, thinking he’s just playing a role. However, the enemies he believes to be imaginary turn out to be very real. What begins as a simple game soon blurs the lines between delusion and reality as his group gains influence and power in the world. As the story progresses, Cid’s followers, unaware of his true motives, unknowingly carry out his plans. They become entangled in a dangerous battle against the cult, unaware that the threats they face are far greater than Cid could have ever imagined. His influence spreads as his actions have more consequences than he realizes. Throughout the series, Cid gradually uncovers the true extent of the cult's power. However, his delusions persist, and he continues to think everything is part of his grand plan. The Eminence in Shadow is an exciting blend of action, comedy, and mystery, with a protagonist who remains unaware of his growing importance.

Major Story Arcs

Shadow's Awakening

In this arc, Cid Kagenou begins to establish himself as a mysterious and powerful figure in the shadows. Believing it is all just an act, he slowly gains followers who are unaware of his true intentions, and his organization begins to take shape. This arc introduces Cid’s dream of becoming a true mastermind, though he remains unaware of how real his actions are becoming.

The Secret Organization Arc

As Cid continues to build his influence, the hidden organization he forms begins to unravel larger plots that threaten the world. However, Cid remains unaware of the true extent of the consequences his actions have on the larger scale. This arc explores the development of his followers and how they are drawn into the mystery of his grand scheme.

The Cult of Diablos

The arc focuses on the rise of a powerful cult and Cid’s battle against it, all while he remains in the shadows, unaware of the impact his actions are having on the cult's rise. Cid’s true identity becomes more critical as the cult threatens everything he has worked for, and he must navigate the growing tensions between his secret life and the dangers surrounding him.

Main Characters

Cid Kagenou (Shadow)

Cid is the eccentric protagonist of The Eminence in Shadow, obsessed with playing the role of a mastermind working from the shadows. While appearing as an average noble in public, he secretly leads Shadow Garden, a group devoted to fighting the Cult of Diablos. Highly skilled in combat and strategy, Cid's theatrical nature often leads him to craft elaborate scenarios—unintentionally uncovering real threats. Despite his brilliance, he remains oblivious to the true influence of his actions.

Alpha

Alpha is the first member of Shadow Garden and Cid's most loyal follower. Originally a cursed elf, she was saved by Cid, who cured her affliction and inspired her unwavering devotion. Graceful and highly intelligent, Alpha serves as the group's de facto leader, ensuring Cid’s plans are flawlessly executed—even when his intentions are misunderstood. Her commanding presence and exceptional combat skills make her a cornerstone of Shadow Garden's success.

Beta

Beta is the second member of Shadow Garden, known for her elegance, literary talent, and devotion to Cid. A skilled swordswoman, she is fiercely loyal yet often competes with Alpha for Cid’s attention. Beta is also the author of a popular in-world novel series inspired by Cid’s random musings, showcasing her creativity and wit. Beneath her refined demeanor lies a sharp, calculating mind and a strong determination to serve Shadow Garden.

Rose Oriana

Rose Oriana is the princess of the Oriana Kingdom, admired for her grace, intellect, and exceptional swordsmanship. A strong-willed and independent leader, she values justice and strives to protect her people. Initially unaware of Shadow Garden's secrets, Rose’s life changes after encountering Cid, whose philosophy deeply influences her. Beneath her royal demeanor lies a conflicted soul, wrestling with the expectations of her position and her growing desire for true freedom.

Alexia Midgar

Alexia Midgar, the rebellious second princess of the Midgar Kingdom, is known for her sharp wit and fiery personality. Despite her royal status, she despises the superficiality of noble life and prefers to pave her own path. An adept swordswoman, she initially views Cid as a mere tool in her schemes but later begins to question her feelings for him. Behind her strong exterior, Alexia hides insecurities stemming from living in her sister's shadow, making her journey one of self-discovery and resilience.

Iris Midgar

Iris Midgar is the crown princess of the Midgar Kingdom and a commander renowned for her unmatched swordsmanship. A symbol of justice and strength, she shoulders the immense responsibility of safeguarding her nation. Her leadership often puts her at odds with her sister Alexia, but her devotion to family remains steadfast. Beneath her composed exterior lies the weight of her duties and high expectations.
